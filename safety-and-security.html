Safety & Security

Last Updated: May 25, 2025

At Developer Hasebur, your safety and the security of your data are paramount. We are committed to protecting the information you share with us and ensuring a secure Browse experience on our website: https://developerhasebur.eu.org/.

1. Secure Data Transmission (SSL)

Our entire website operates under HTTPS protocol, indicated by the padlock icon in your browser's address bar. This means all communication between your browser and our server is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. This helps prevent unauthorized access to any information you send or receive from our site, such as contact form submissions.

2. Data Protection Measures

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information. These measures include data encryption, secure server environments, and restricted access to data to authorized personnel only.

3. Payment Security (For future services/products)

While this is primarily a portfolio site, if we ever offer direct services or products requiring payment, we will ensure that all payment transactions are processed through secure, PCI DSS compliant third-party payment gateways. We do not store sensitive payment card details on our servers.

4. User Responsibility

While we take extensive measures to secure our site, user vigilance also plays a role in online safety:

  • Strong Passwords: If any part of our site requires account creation (e.g., for a client portal), always use strong, unique passwords.
  • Beware of Phishing: Be cautious of suspicious emails or messages claiming to be from Developer Hasebur. We will never ask for your password or sensitive information via email.
  • Keep Software Updated: Ensure your operating system and web browser are updated to the latest versions to benefit from the most recent security patches.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use reputable third-party services (e.g., website hosting, analytics tools) that also adhere to high security standards. We carefully select our partners to ensure they prioritize data protection.

6. Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we have procedures in place to promptly identify, contain, and address the incident, as well as notify affected individuals and authorities as required by law.

7. Continuous Monitoring

We continuously monitor our website for potential vulnerabilities and implement updates and patches as needed to maintain a high level of security.

8. Contact Us Regarding Security Concerns

If you have any questions or concerns about the security of our website, or if you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately at developerhasebur@gmail.com. Your feedback helps us improve our security posture.
